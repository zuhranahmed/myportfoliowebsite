ABOUT ME

Hey there! my name is Zuhran Ahmed. I am a self-taught front-end developer and digital media marketer. I started my journey with programming back in 2016 when I just found out that there's a whole universe of logic behind everything we see online which we achieve through programming.

So, I started searching for different programming languages and ended up with Python. I took a course online and learned about it. I did nothing with Python afterwords. At that time I was just jumping from one language to another without any clear plan and goal. I also learned Java and C++ which I also didn't use in any practical way.

I was naive and I procrastinated a lot from 2019 to mid-2020. In the mid-2020 month of June, I decided that I'm going to learn Front-end Development. I took online courses for HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I learned them thoroughly and start practicing. I am still learning and practicing to improve my skills as programming is a never-ending journey. I also learned Sass and TailwindCSS. Besides that, I also took Google's certification for Digital Marketing where I learned about online marketing, SEO, and how to make SEO friendly websites. I completed that certification with an over 80% score in their online final exam.

That's my journey so far which I'll continue to improve for the rest of my life. I'm very passionate about what I do and what I learn. I always want to learn new stuff and improve my skills.
